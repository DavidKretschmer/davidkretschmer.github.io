Welcome! I am a Postdoctoral Prize Research Fellow at Nuffield College, University of Oxford.

My research interests are in the fields of intergroup relations, immigrant social and cultural integration, and social network analysis.

The comment is a methodological critique of Smith et al.'s 2016 paper on [Ethnic Composition and Friendship Segregation: Differential Effects for Adolescent Natives and Immigrants](https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/doi/full/10.1086/684032). Be sure to also read Smith et al.'s [reply](https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/doi/10.1086/727858)
